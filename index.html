Privacy Policy

App Name: MindShelf

Developer: Ideloc Studio

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

At Ideloc Studio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how MindShelf collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless synchronization experience, we collect limited personal information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and encrypted credentials when you create an account.
  • User Content: Bookmarks, notes, and organizational data you explicitly save within the app.
  • Technical Data: Basic device information and crash logs provided via Apple to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used strictly to create and manage your account, sync your data securely across devices, and provide core app functionality. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

User data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure. All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard cryptographic protocols, and data transmission is protected via SSL/TLS encryption.

4. Purchases and Subscriptions

All financial transactions and subscriptions are processed securely by Apple’s App Store. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card or billing details.

5. Notifications

If enabled, the app may send local reminders or push notifications. No notification content is stored externally beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.

6. Data Deletion and Rights

You have the right to access or delete your data at any time. You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app settings, or by contacting us via email.

7. Third-Party Services

MindShelf uses Apple StoreKit and Supabase, which operate under their own respective privacy policies.
