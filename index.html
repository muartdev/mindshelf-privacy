Privacy Policy – MindShelf

Effective Date: 2026

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and encrypted password. We also store the bookmarks you choose to save inside the app.

How We Use Information

Your information is used only to create and manage your account, sync bookmarks securely, and provide core app functionality. We do not sell or share your data.

Data Storage

User data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure. Passwords are encrypted.

Purchases

All subscriptions are processed by Apple’s App Store. We do not access payment details.

Notifications

If enabled, the app may send local reminders. No notification data is stored externally.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by contacting: support@mindshelf.app

Third-Party Services

MindShelf uses Apple StoreKit and Supabase, which operate under their own privacy policies.

Contact

Email: support@mindshelf.app
